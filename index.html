Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cookies!

Here at Pat's Salmon Cookies, we are passionate about making delicious sweets as well as advocating for healthy ecosystems. Our cookies are made using locally sourced ingredients from growers who are mindful of the environment and what goes into their products. We offer a large variety of salmon shapes, but if you don't see the species that you are looking for, you can contact us and we will be happy to customize them for you! Our Salmon Cookies can be enjoyed guilt-free by humans and salmon alike!
